How to Calculate Your GPA & CGPA

A Grade Point Average (GPA) is a numerical representation of your academic performance. Our free online calculator helps you track both semester GPA and cumulative CGPA across multiple semesters with ease.

Understanding the Grading Scale

Indian universities typically use a 10-point grading scale where O (Outstanding) = 10, A+ = 9, A = 8, B+ = 7, B = 6, and C = 5. Simply enter your course credits and grades to get instant, accurate results.

  • Mobile Friendly: Works perfectly on all devices

How GPA Calculation Works

GPA is calculated by multiplying each course's credit hours by the grade points earned, then dividing the total grade points by the total credit hours. Our calculator automates this process, ensuring accuracy and saving you time.
