10-Point Grading Scale for Indian Universities

Most Indian universities use a 10-point grading scale where O (Outstanding) = 9.0-10.0, D+ (Excellent) = 8.0-8.9, D (Distinction) = 7.5-7.9, A+ (Very Good) = 7.0-7.4, A (Good) = 6.0-6.9, B (Average) = 5.0-5.9, C (Satisfactory) = 4.0-4.9, and U (Reappear/Absent) = 0.0. How to Calculate GPA and CGPA

GPA (Grade Point Average) is calculated by multiplying each course's credit hours by the grade points earned, then dividing the total grade points by the total credit hours. CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is calculated the same way but includes all semesters. Our calculator automates this process for Indian university students, ensuring 100% accuracy and saving you time during exam results.
